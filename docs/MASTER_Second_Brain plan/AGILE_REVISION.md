# Agile Startup Revision — 12 Core Roles (Not 30)

## Philosophy

**Startup culture > Corporate structure.**  
We want **agile, multi-role employees** who:
- Wear multiple hats
- Focus on outcomes, not titles
- Autonomously ship value
- Learn and adapt weekly

### Why This Works

- **12 roles** instead of 30 → manageable, agile, startup-friendly
- Each role is **multi-functional** → employees wear multiple hats
- **P0 roles (9)** can run the entire business alone: 01–04 (Bej), 06–09 (Brentford, Altair, Rockerforce), 11 (Automation)
- **P1 roles (3)** are growth levers: 05 (UX), 10 (Marketing), 12 (CFO/CTO)
- No silos → everyone understand the whole business
