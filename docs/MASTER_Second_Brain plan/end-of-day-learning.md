# Workflow: End-of-Day Learning Note (Employee 01)

**Trigger phrases**:  
- "end of day learning"  
- "daily learning"  
- "what did we learn today?"

**Schedule**: Daily 6:00 PM CEST via Hermes cron

**Agent**: Employee 01 (BejCapital Research & Distribution Operator)

**Prompt**:

## Context

Read:
- `MASTER/05-HERMES/HERMES.md`
- `MASTER/05-HERMES/agents/employee-01/EMPLOYEE_01.md`
- Today's brief (`MASTER/00-INBOX/brief-YYYY-MM-DD.md`)
- Today's inbox triage (`MASTER/05-HERMES/agents/employee-01/inbox-triage/YYYY-MM-DD.md`)
- Today's social drafts (`MASTER/05-HERMES/agents/employee-01/social-drafts/YYYY-MM-DD/`)
- Recent notes in `MASTER/01-WIKI/` (last 7 days)

## Task

Write a **learning note** capturing:

### 1. Patterns Appeared Today

- What patterns appeared in your captures, research, or emails?
- What themes are emerging across different domains?
- What is your brain clearly working on?

### 2. Learning About My Thinking

- What did you learn about b.'s thinking today?
- What beliefs were confirmed or challenged?
- What contradictions between today's notes and past beliefs?

### 3. What To Do Differently Tomorrow

- What should Employee 01 do differently tomorrow?
- What prompts or workflows should be adjusted?
- What information should be gathered more aggressively?

### 4. What b. Should Think About More

- What is b. clearly not thinking about enough?
- What gaps exist in today's captures/research?
- What question should b. sit with tomorrow?

## Output Format

Save as `MASTER/05-HERMES/agents/employee-01/learning/YYYY-MM-DD.md`:

```markdown
# Learning Note — YYYY-MM-DD

## Patterns Appeared Today

- ...

## Learning About b.'s Thinking

- ...

## What Employee 01 Should Do Differently Tomorrow

- ...

## What b. Should Think About More

- ...
```

Post a 100–150 word summary to Discord #hermes-chat.
